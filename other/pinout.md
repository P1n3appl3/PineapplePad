## Pinout

- Port A
	- A0: U0Rx
	- A1: U0Tx
	- A2: Sclk SPI clock from microcontroller to TFT or SDC
	- A3: TFT_CS, active low to enable TFT
	- A4: MISO SPI data from SDC to microcontroller
	- A5: MOSI SPI data from microcontroller to TFT or SDC
	- A6: TFT data/command
	- A7: TFT reset
- Port B
	- B0:

    - B7: Shorted with D1

	- C4: U1Rx
	- C5: U1Tx
	- C6:

- Port D
	- D0: Shorted with B6
	- D1: Shorted with B7
	- D2: ADC in (A5)
	- D3:
	- D6:
	- D7: SDC_CS
- Port E
	- E0:
	- E1:
	- E2:
	- E3:
	- E4:
	- E5: Audio Out
- Port F
	- F0: Onboard Button 2
	- F1: RED_LED
	- F2: BLUE_LED
	- F3: GREEN_LED
	- F4: Onboard Button 1
